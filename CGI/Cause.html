Monopoly, its not a game.

In the capitalistic world, monopoly represents as a "cheat" in the system. It occurs when one seller or producer gains the largest control over other competitors in a particular sector or industry. Additionally, monopoly power is controlling the prices in the market also this power allows maintain prices above marginal costs.

Causes in monopolization

Usually, big tech companies conducted monopolistic reason in dominance. Some of these activities include:

Anti-competitive Acquisitions: In neutralizing growing threats from startups, acquisitions is used as a strategy.

Control Over Market Platforms: Companies have been criticized for using their platform dominance to favor their own products over those of other sellers.

Bundling and Tying: Tech giants use their dominance in one area to force entry into another or bundle products to stifle competition.

Data Dominance: Big tech firms often control vast amounts of user data, which they can use to gain an unfair advantage over competitors.
