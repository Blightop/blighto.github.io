Just to break it up?

Breaking up big-tech could result in several ways:

Promotion of Competition: More companies can establish in the market without any limitations.

Stimulation of Innovation: Startups may thrive and innovate in the market.

Consumer welfare: More choices, services and prices to develop.

Essentially, the aforementioned points are elements in creating a capitalistic environment. Therefore, capitalism act will be highly promoted!

Yet, there are repercussion of such an action...

Issues

Dropbacks in breaking up Big-tech:

Economic Efficiency: Lower efficiency and higher operational cost for Broken up Companies owing to economies of scale.

Global Competitiveness: Most big-tech companies are created in the US. Weakening them will result in foreign competitors, with potential implications in technology leadership and national security.

Innovation risk: Less investment in innovative ideas, which disrupts develops on technological advancements.

Views from consumers?

Throughout this debate, experts found that consumers still benefited from the services provided by big tech companies. Contributed factors included efficiency, integration and cost effectiveness. The only significant concerns in this situation is privacy and data security.

However, breaking up was predicted to increase fears of:

Reduced functionality

High cost of services
